Building a page with "tables" can give you
a nice professional look. Notice how this page
looks like a page built in frames.

"but it's not in frames !"

There are some options you can fool with.
You can adjust the widths or a add
background color to the table cells
to customize your page.

A sample table cell with added bgcolor.
<td width="15%" align="center" valign="top" bgcolor="lightcyan">
